Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Bible Devotional AI ("we," "our," or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure environment for your scholarly spiritual journey.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized study experience, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication.
  • User Content: We store your saved studies, library items, highlights, and (for premium users) prayer journal entries in Google Cloud Firestore to enable cross-device synchronization.
  • Economic Data: We track your AI credit balance and transaction history (logs) to manage your daily allowances and premium subscriptions.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous device identifiers and basic usage statistics to improve app performance and stability.

2. Third-Party Services

Our App utilizes professional third-party services to deliver high-quality content and secure payments:

  • OpenAI: Your study topics and verse selections are processed by OpenAI to generate scholarly theological insights. We do not share your personal email or identity with OpenAI.
  • RevenueCat & Google Play Store: All subscription payments are processed securely through RevenueCat and the Google Play billing system. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.
  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Your profile and study data are stored on secure Google Cloud servers.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is physically retained for as long as your account is active. You have the "Right to be Forgotten" and may request account deletion at any time:

  • In-App Deletion: Navigate to Settings > Delete Account to physically purge your records instantly.
  • Manual Request: Contact us at support@fajostech.com for manual processing.

For more details, please see our full Data Deletion Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

Bible Devotional AI is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
